Triskell Summer Seminar 2013

Julien Richard-Foy

Energy-aware Software Engineering

The Problem

  • Energy becomes scarce and is polluting

  • Computers represent 15% of our electricity bill 1

  • Energy consumption is a cross-cutting and non-functional concern

  • Experiments showed that software energy consumption can vary from 1 to 100 according to their implementation 2

  • How to build energy-efficient softwares?

Solutions

  • Embrace diversity: generate software variants optimized according to their environment

  • Compilers should help us designing efficient softwares
